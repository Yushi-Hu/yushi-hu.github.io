Hi! I am a first year PhD student at the University of Washington (UW). I am working in the UW NLP group, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Mari Ostendorf. I am primarily interested in Natural Language Processing and Speech Processing. I am now focusing on conversational AI. For example, how to leverage the power of big pretrained language models (GPT3, Codex) when dealing with dialogues? how to ground external knowledge in dialogue understanding and response generation?

Prior to my PhD, I spent four wonderful years at the University of Chicago and got bachelor's degree in mathematics, computer science, and economics. In Chicago, I was advised by Prof. Karen Livescu at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC), where I worked on self-supervised learning of speech, solving tasks like speech recognition and keyword detection.

Before focusing on NLP and ML, I also helped applying computational methods in physics. I worked with Dr. Saidur Bakaul at Argonne National Laboratory and Prof. Chuangang Ning at Tsinghua University.
